PokeSquad Builder

Site Plan Documentation

1. Site Name & Branding

Name: PokeSquad Builder

Why?

  • "Poke" instantly signals Pokémon content.
  • "Squad" implies team-building (friendly, competitive vibe).
  • "Builder" clarifies functionality (not just a database).
  • Short, memorable, and SEO-friendly for Pokémon fans.

2. Site Purpose

What It Does:

  • Helps Pokémon trainers build balanced teams for competitive battles.
  • Fetches real-time Pokémon stats, moves, and type data from PokéAPI.
  • Provides instant feedback on team weaknesses/strengths.
  • Lets users save/share teams for collaboration.

Target Audience:

  • Competitive Pokémon players (VGC, Smogon tiers).
  • Casual fans planning in-game teams.
  • Content creators sharing strategies.

3. User Scenarios

Scenario 1: Competitive Player

"I need a Fire-type Pokémon for my team, but I don't want to double up on weaknesses. Can PokeSquad Builder show me which Fire-types cover my team's gaps?"

Content Solution:

  • Type-coverage calculator highlighting team vulnerabilities.
  • Filter Fire-types by secondary typing (e.g., Charizard's Flying resists Ground).

Scenario 2: Casual Fan

"I'm playing Pokémon Scarlet and want a balanced story-mode team. How do I know if my squad is well-rounded?"

Content Solution:

  • Pre-built "Beginner's Guide" templates (e.g., "Story Mode Balanced Team").
  • Simple color-coded weakness chart (red = critical gap).
